About the Author

Gianmaria Giannini is an independent researcher and specialist in the engineering of marine renewables, with a consolidated scientific identity focused on wave and tidal energy technologies. He currently is a Marie Sklodowska-Curie Actions (MSCA) Postdoctoral fellow at Marine Offhore Renewable Energy (MORE) lab of the Politecnico di Torino, where he is the Principal Investigator for the WEC-Pro project. This initiative operates at the cutting edge of the sector, exploring the intersection of hydrodynamic optimization and Large-Scale Additive Manufacturing (LSAM) for Wave Energy Converters in partnership with global robotics leader Caracol AM and, with research entities, such as the Interdishiplinary Centre of Marine Environmental Research (CIIMAR), the Faculty of Engineering of Univeristy of Porto(FEUP), Portugal, and the Natural Ocean Engineering Laboratory (NOEL), Italy.

With a background that bridges advanced academia and high-tech industry, Gianmaria has held key research and specialist roles across the UK, Italy, and Portugal. His work has been recognized with multiple innovation awards, and he has authored over 60 scientific papers and technical reports. Holding a PhD in Naval Architecture, Ocean and Marine Engineering from the University of Strathclyde, Gianmaria remains dedicated to fostering synergies between research institutions and industrial partners to advance sustainable marine technologies.
